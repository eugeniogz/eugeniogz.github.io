About the app

The journal that learns from you

Wingene VIDA is a reflective journal built upon the VIDA Ethical-Existential Method — a philosophical practice for personal improvement developed in the Wingene project. More than a digital notebook, the app transforms your everyday notes into a mirror of patterns, trends, and growth opportunities.

Artificial intelligence analyzes your own records to generate insights and practical advice — without ever exposing your data or using it to train models.

May our brief passage be more than a crossing — beats that echo in the universal music. — Wingene Manifest

The four pillars

The MethodVIDA

V

First pillar

Values

The moral compass. Identifying and reinforcing the principles that guide your choices — personal boundaries, coexistence, and continuous review.

I

Second pillar

Imperfections

Signals, not guilt. Recognizing areas for improvement with compassion — personal, emotional, moral, and attentional imperfections.

I

Third pillar

Decisions

Purpose in motion. Observing choice patterns — reactive, constructive, and adaptive decisions over time.

A

Fourth pillar

Attention

Conscious presence. Cultivating attention to oneself, to others, and to the world — the practice that transforms the ordinary into learning.

Features

One practice, multiple dimensions

  • 📝

    Daily journaling

    Write down everyday perceptions, actions, and difficulties without judgment. The app suggests classifications within the method's pillars.

  • 🤖

    AI Analysis

    Artificial intelligence (Gemini) reads your accounts and provides practical philosophical insights. Completely anonymous queries — never used to train models.

  • 📊

    VIDA Radar and Heatmap

    Track the distribution of records across the four pillars and visualize your practice consistency over time.

  • 🌐

    Offline first

    Works perfectly offline, syncing automatically with Google Drive as soon as the network is restored.

Privacy and security

Your data is exclusively yours

Wingene VIDA was built with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought.

🔒 End-to-end encryption. All data is encrypted using a key generated from your personal password — which never leaves your device.

☁️ Personal Google Drive. Backups are stored in a hidden folder within your own account — inaccessible to developers or third parties.

🤖 Anonymous AI. Queries to the artificial intelligence are made in an anonymized mode and never used to train foundational models.

📵 Biometrics and PIN. Support for biometric or device PIN lock for additional local protection.
